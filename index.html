About

My name is Bassem Debbabi. I am a PhD Student at the University of Grenoble, advised by Pr. Philippe Lalanda and Dr. Ada Diaconescu., within Adèle research group of LIG Laboratory (Laboratoire d'Informatique de Grenoble).

I have received Msc in Computer Science from Joseph Fourier University of Grenoble (France), under the supervision of Pr. Philippe Lalanda.

I have received Engineering diploma in computer science from University of Sétif (Algeria), under the supervision of Djamila Mechta.
